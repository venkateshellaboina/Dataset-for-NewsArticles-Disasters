A NOTORIOUS volcano spewing smoke and ash into the air grounded flights less than an hour after a group of 14 Warwick residents landed in the country and their return trip remains up in the air.

Jack Campbell was on the last flight able to land in Bali on Thursday with his family and friends, just after the eruption of Mt Agung shut down Denpasar Airport.

The group is celebrating Mr Campbell's 18th birthday hadn't even made it to their resort, located 27km south of Mt Agung, when they received news the airport had been closed.
