In a massive fire that broke out at the four-storey Hotel Arpit Palace in central Delhi’s Karol Bagh in the early hours of Tuesday, 17 people were killed,
 including three Myanmar nationals, and three sustained injuries. The fire started around 2.30 a.m. but the fire service was alerted two hours later.
 About 25 fire tenders were pressed into service as soon as the call was received, and 35 people were rescued.
