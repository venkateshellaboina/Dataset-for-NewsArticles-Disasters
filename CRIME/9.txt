Three members of a family were found murdered early today morning in New Delhi's posh Vasant Kunj neighbourhood. The murdered include Mithlesh Verma, his wife Siya and daughter Neha. Mithlesh's son was found injured.

The husband, wife and their daughter were killed using a kitchen knife, police said. The triple murder came to light after Mithlesh's son raised an alarm at around 4am today morning.

The neighbour's heard the son's cries and spotted the three bodies lying in the Verams' home, following which they alerted the police.
