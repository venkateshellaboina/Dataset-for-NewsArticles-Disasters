 As many as 71 people were killed across the country on Sunday as lightning and thunderstorms hit five states, throwing life out of gear and leaving behind
a trail of destruction. Deaths were reported from Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal and Delhi-NCR. At least 42 people were killed and 84 injured in hail and thunderstorm that hit different parts of Uttar Pradesh.
Lakhimpur Kheri saw the maximum damage with 84 houses and buildings collapsing partly or completely in the storm, followed by Sambhal where 31 buildings were
damaged. A total of 121 houses were reported damaged in the storm.
